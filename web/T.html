Transformers

Transformers is a 2007 American science fiction action film based on the toy line of the same name created by Hasbro.

It is the first installment in the Transformers film series, and was directed by Michael Bay and produced by Paramount Pictures.

The film stars Shia LaBeouf as Sam Witwicky, a teenager who gets caught up in a war between the heroic Autobots and the villainous Decepticons, two factions of alien robots who can disguise themselves by transforming into everyday machinery.
